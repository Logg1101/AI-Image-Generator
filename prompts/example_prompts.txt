# Example Prompts for AI Image Generator

## 1. Photorealistic Portrait
Prompt:
"photorealistic portrait of a young woman, soft lighting, 8k resolution, cinematic color grading, sharp focus, detailed skin texture"

Negative Prompt:
"blurry, lowres, deformed hands, extra fingers, bad anatomy, low detail, oversaturated"

---

## 2. Cyberpunk Cityscape
Prompt:
"futuristic cyberpunk city at night, glowing neon lights, flying cars, rainy atmosphere, cinematic, ultra-detailed, 4k"

Negative Prompt:
"lowres, dull lighting, foggy, out of frame, distorted buildings, watermark"

---

## 3. Anime Character
Prompt:
"anime-style girl with long silver hair, futuristic armor, glowing eyes, standing on a spaceship deck, full body, dynamic pose, detailed background"

Negative Prompt:
"blurry, extra limbs, poor anatomy, text watermark, distorted face"
